## Notes on referencing

- Websites without publication dates use *n.d.* (no date) per AU Harvard convention.
- Access dates use *viewed* followed by the date the resource was accessed.
- URLs are enclosed in angle brackets in the reference list.
- In-text citations would appear as (Inspired by Iceland n.d.), (WebAIM 2026), or (Ellis, K n.d.) depending on context.
